PLAYER SPOTLIGHT

Lamine Yamal

2024/25 Season

  • Appearances: 55
  • Goals: 18
  • Assists: 21

Born in Mataró to a Moroccan father and Equatorial Guinean mother, his talent was undeniable from the moment he joined La Masia at age 7. The 2024/25 season marked his coronation. With Barcelona trailing in El Clásico, the teenager curled a golazo past Courtois, becoming the youngest scorer in the fixture’s history. By season’s end, he’d broken Ansu Fati’s records for youngest Champions League starter (16y 83d), La Liga assist leader (17 in a season), and Golden Boy winner.
